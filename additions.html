Here you can meet some secondary characters!

Chloe Park

Chloe Park is an ancillary character in We Bare Bears. She is a Korean girl who snuck into the Bears' cave to gather information for her college report before eventually befriending them. Chloe skipped Junior High to University, as she is a child prodigy, and is currently studying at the University of California.

Charlie

Charles better known as Charlie is a Bigfoot who is a recurring character in We Bare Bears. Charlie is characterized as loud and eccentric. He always stays positive throughout the day. The Bears meet him when Charlie signs up on a home-sharing website to spend a weekend with the Bears. Charlie is the worst possible house guest for the Bears and nearly destroys their house.

Nom Nom

Nom Nom is a recurring character and the main antagonist during the two first seasons. He is a multi-billionaire anthropomorphic koala who had acquired internet fame through a series of popular videos on the internet. Nom Nom is characterized as greedy and competitive. He says he doesn't have time for hanging out with friends in real life, and he has what he needs: fame, money and the internet.
